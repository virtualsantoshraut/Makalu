Makalu , A Reputed School in Sankhuwasabha

Makalu Arun School stands tall as a significant educational hub in the Sankhuwasabha district, nestled within Chainpur Municipality. For the rural communities yearning for English medium education for their children, this institution has been nothing short of a boon. The school's core mission revolves around ensuring the 'right to education' for every child while focusing on their holistic development. With an emphasis on practical education and a child-friendly environment, Makalu Arun School has carved its place as a fruitful and nurturing educational establishment.


Established in the year 2065 BS, Makalu Arun School's primary objective has been to provide quality education to all, without any discrimination. Under the able guidance of its principal, Mr. Sundar Kumar Shrestha, the school has embarked on a decade-long journey, nurturing and molding hundreds of students for a promising future. The school's educational journey has garnered immense praise, and its students have emerged as strong contenders, even when competing with students from reputed urban schools. In recent years, and most notably this year, Makalu Arun School achieved the best results in the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) throughout the district. Currently, the school boasts an enrollment of approximately 350 students, eager to partake in its enriching academic environment.


The crux of Makalu Arun School's success lies in its effective teaching and learning process. The teachers, highly qualified and experienced with exemplary academic qualifications, have mastered the art of catering to students' unique psychological understandings. While academic excellence remains a priority, the school's focus extends beyond that to encompass the all-round development of its students. To facilitate their studies, the school provides a well-equipped library, a state-of-the-art science lab, and a computer hall. Moreover, Makalu Arun School believes in practical education and regularly organizes scheduled educational tours, field visits, science exhibitions, and other hands-on experiences.


Every Friday, the school takes a break from the conventional routine and engages in weekly extra-curricular activities to boost students' overall performance. These events include dance competitions, quiz contests, debate competitions, spelling contests, aptitude tests, and various indoor and outdoor sports activities. Such activities not only add an element of fun to the learning process but also instill valuable life skills in the students.


A child-friendly environment is the cornerstone of modern education, and Makalu Arun School has taken this principle to heart. To ensure a conducive learning environment, the school has separate buildings and playgrounds for junior and senior students. It also provides essential amenities such as a well-maintained canteen, clean washrooms/toilets, and access to pure drinking water. Safety is of utmost importance, and students are closely monitored to prevent any accidents. The school also emphasizes values like cleanliness, teamwork, dutifulness, honesty, and responsiveness while nurturing a love for nature.


One remarkable aspect that sets Makalu Arun School apart from others is its commitment to maintaining an English-speaking environment at all times. The students are immersed in an English-language atmosphere, fostering their language skills from an early age, and preparing them for a globalized world.


In conclusion, Makalu Arun School stands as a well-managed institution, offering abundant services with a focus on education rather than profits. For a decade, it has illuminated the path of education, cutting through the darkness of ignorance and transforming the lives of countless children. Despite facing challenges and shortcomings, the school remains steadfast in its dedication to providing the best education to its students using all available means.


Makalu Arun School serves as a shining example of how education can shape young minds and create a brighter future for the community and the country at large. With its continuous efforts to promote academic excellence, all-round development, and a child-friendly environment, it is a beacon of hope and progress in the field of education.
