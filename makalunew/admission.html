Admission

Makalu Arun School warmly extends its admission process to students from nursery to class 9, catering to a wide age range and promoting holistic education. Aspiring students seeking enrollment are required to furnish their previous academic transcripts, which offer valuable insights into their educational background. This prerequisite allows the school to understand the strengths and needs of each student, enabling personalized support throughout their academic journey.

To secure admission, candidates are also requested to submit a specified admission fee at the school's office room. The school strives to maintain a fair and affordable fee structure, ensuring that education remains accessible to all. Moreover, the amount of both admission and monthly fees is tailored according to the grade level the student is enrolling in, making it reasonable and proportional to the educational services and facilities provided.

With a transparent and straightforward admission process, Makalu Arun School aims to create a nurturing and inclusive learning environment. By valuing the individual strengths of each student and offering quality education, the school fosters a community of passionate learners, empowering them to grow academically and personally as they progress through their educational journey.
