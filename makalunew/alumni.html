Manish Raut

Manish Raut, a bright student, finished his class 10 studies at Makalu Arun School before enrolling in Times International College. His achievements reflect his dedication and passion for physics and academic excellence.

He completed his class 10 education at Makalu Arun School, demonstrating his commitment to academic excellence. His outstanding performance in the exams highlights his hard work and determination, making him a source of inspiration for his peers and educators alike.

Sabin Siwa

Sabin Siwa, a talented student at Makalu Arun School, is a shining star in the district's music scene. Sabin's musical prowess serves as an inspiration to aspiring musicians and reflects his dedication and passion for his craft.
