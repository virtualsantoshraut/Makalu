Why Makalu Arun School?

Sending a child to Makalu Arun School offers a multitude of compelling reasons. Firstly, the school boasts a highly experienced teaching staff with more than 15 years of expertise in their respective fields. This guarantees a top-notch education and personalized guidance for every student. Moreover, the school maintains a low faculty-to-student ratio, ensuring that each child receives individual attention and support, leading to enhanced learning outcomes. The school also places great importance on extracurricular activities (ECA), allowing students to develop their talents and interests beyond academics. In addition to a robust academic curriculum, the school offers excellent facilities, creating an ideal environment for holistic growth and development. One of the most noteworthy aspects is that all these exceptional features come at an affordable price in today's competitive market, making Makalu Arun School an attractive and prudent choice for parents seeking the best education for their child.
